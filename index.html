Nearest towns are Palakkad(35 km), Mannarkkad(15 km), Ottappalam (25 km) and Shoranur (35 km). IEEE Malabar Hub

×
IEEE Malabar Hub was the first subsection to be formed under IEEE Kerala Section. Aiming for better networking between professional members and student members of IEEE in the northern districts of Kerala, the hub started its functioning in Oct 2010, on IEEE day and it remains to be one among the most active subsections in India.

Malabar Hub Meet

×
The Malabar Hub Meet is the arch event of the IEEE Malabar Hub, which features informative workshops, engaging sessions and interactive talks. Attended by over 200 students spanning across different colleges in the Malabar region, this event facilitates a platform for creating building blocks of lifelong and diverse professional network.
